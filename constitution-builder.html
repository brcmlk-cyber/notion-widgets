Constitution Builder

Answer questions about your relationship in four areas. Claude drafts your living Relationship Constitution — your shared values, promises, and agreements in writing.

◈ Who Are We?
Start with names. These appear in the final document. "Partner X" and "Partner Y" work fine if you prefer.
◈ What We Stand For
Values that define how you want to move through life together. These aren't rules — they're the character of the relationship you're building.
Write what actually matters to you, not what sounds ideal.
◈ What We Promise
Promises are not conditions. They are commitments each person makes — independently — about how they will show up.
◈ What We Protect
Limits protect the relationship, not just individuals. These are the lines that define the container — not to restrict, but to give the relationship shape.
◈ Ready to Generate
Claude will synthesize your answers into a living Relationship Constitution — drafted to feel like yours, not a template. You can refine it afterward.
